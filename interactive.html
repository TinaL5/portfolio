Interactive Design


Quality Assurance at Air Canada

Role as human performance technologist with eLearning and Knowledge Management

Acceptance testing
of a learning system

Launch excerpt of instrument

The instrument design focussed on human-computer interaction, specfically system functionality and usability.

Criteria targetting learning design were included in the acceptance process.

Testing was performed prior to software release to decide acceptance or rejection of the product.

Task analyses for six user groups were conducted and served as scenarios during testing.

Leard, T. (2002). Knowledge management and system design: Examining practices at Air Canada (Unpublished master's internship report). Concordia University.
