Student models

Launch presentation paper

Intelligent systems interpret interactions through modelling and then respond accordingly.

This model of decision pathways that I designed comprises dialogues represented with letters and possible decisions represented with numbers.

Decisions aligned to an expert model are coded as positive while other decisions are coded as negative. Feedback is targetted on positive and negative outcomes. The system is deterministic with finite memory. Feedback is adjusted depending on accumulative outcomes. Critical thinking skills are mapped to the decisions.
